Summary: NYC vs SF Bike Share Analysis

Team Yowza decided to analyze the bike share programs in New York and San Francisco, comparing the typical biker profile and bike usage in the two cities. While a fair comparison between the two cities could not be conducted, due to data availability and time constraints, it was interesting to see the differences between the two places.

Overall, both cities lagged behind in female users, this is believed to be due to women tending to be more safety conscious. There is also a definite peak during work rush hours, more heavily weighted to the afternoon rush hour.
